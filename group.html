VIETNAM

We are excited to announce our presence in Vietnam through two esteemed entities, BoilerMaster Vietnam Co., Ltd, and Trident Hydro Jetting Co., Ltd. BoilerMaster Vietnam, incorporated in September 2014, operates from an extensive 1.5-hectare facility in the VSIP, Quang Ngai Industrial Park, Dung Quat Economic Zone, Quang Ngai Province. Our services encompass mechanical and maintenance services for refineries, power stations, and utilities, including turn-around activities, along with the construction, repair, and maintenance of heat transfer equipment such as boilers and heat exchangers. We offer comprehensive inspection services, water jetting, refractory and insulation supply, scaffoldings, chemical cleaning, pigging, and related services.

In addition to BoilerMaster Vietnam, we are proud to introduce Trident Hydro Jetting Co., Ltd, located in the Nghi Son Economic Zone, Thanh Hoa Province, Vietnam. Our expansion into Vietnam reflects our unwavering commitment to providing exceptional industrial solutions to this dynamic market.

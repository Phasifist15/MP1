About Us

Who We Are

Trident Hydro is a reputable company that specializes in catering to the specific needs of shipyards, power plants, and oil and gas companies in the Philippines. With our extensive experience and unwavering commitment to excellence, we have firmly established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry.

What We Do

Our primary focus at Trident Hydro revolves around the design and fabrication of heat exchangers, designed to meet the rigorous demands of our clients. Additionally, we offer a range of valuable services, including the retubing of boilers and heat exchangers. We pride ourselves on providing plant shutdown operation assistance, ensuring the smooth and efficient management of critical maintenance processes. Moreover, our high-pressure jetting services are designed to meet the most challenging cleaning and maintenance requirements, all tailored to deliver exceptional results.

Mission

Our mission at Trident Hydro is to be the cornerstone of sustainable progress in the shipyard, power plant, and oil and gas industries of the Philippines. We aim to provide innovative and reliable heat exchanger solutions, along with a comprehensive suite of services, to enhance the efficiency and longevity of our clients' operations. By relentlessly pursuing excellence, we seek to contribute to the success of our partners while promoting a safer and cleaner environment.

Vision

Trident Hydro envisions a future where our products and services play a pivotal role in powering the growth of maritime, energy, and petrochemical sectors throughout the Philippines. We aspire to be the leading name associated with cutting-edge heat exchanger design and fabrication, pioneering the industry with environmentally conscious practices. Our vision is to foster enduring partnerships, drive technological advancements, and create a legacy of excellence that transcends borders and stands as a testament to our unwavering commitment to progress.
